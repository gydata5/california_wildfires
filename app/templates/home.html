Welcome to the California Wildfire Data Dashboard. Here you can find insights on wildfire activities in California.

This project analyzes California wildfire data from 2013 to 2019, highlighting their increasing prevalence and the devastating impacts on land, human life, wildlife, air quality, and local economies.
