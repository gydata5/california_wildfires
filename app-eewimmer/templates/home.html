Welcome to the California Wildfire Data Dashboard. Here you can find insights on wildfire activities in California.
Wildfires are becoming more and more prevalent in California; causing more loss of land, human life, wildlife, and wildlife habitat. Areas impacted by wildfires see negative impacts on air quality, surrounding temperatures, and local economies.

Top 5 Causes of Wildfires

  1. California's climate
  2. Strong winds
  3. Geography and vegetation
  4. Human activity
  5. Climate change
